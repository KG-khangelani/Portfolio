The problem

As effective as they get to be at thier best posters now are slowly becoming a thing of the past. Everyone now is focusing on thier smartphones. Apps have taken over.

The brief

For this brief I had to redesign the poster from the previous brief to make it detectable when scanned. Then render a video that will pop when the user interacts with the poster using the ROAR app. With souund included. I need to make use of Illustrator, After-Effects and the ROAR platform.

The Solution

Thanks to the evolution of technology, now there's Argumented Reality(AR) technology. This has put still-life such as posters into motion, giving them to life thus bringing us immersive interactive experinces.
I designed my video elemnts using Illustrator. Then set the elemnts on motion using After-Effects. For composition and layering I then used the ROAR online platform.
