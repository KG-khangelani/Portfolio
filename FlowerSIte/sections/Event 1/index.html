Poster Design

For this brief I had to design a typographically driven poster of a sentence made up of seven words. With each word choosen from a different category to construct a powerful and self motivational sentence that will serve as the textual foundation of the poster.

The words have been divided into seven categories namely: Fear, Encouragement, Lust, Anger, Greed, Safety and Forbidden.
