The Brief

Adoption is a beautiful thing to do. The issue is that the process takes so long and having to travel to-and-fro to the center can be a mission. With sometimes not even getting the right dog that one likes. As intervention an app has to be designed to help make the process easier than it is currently. An app that will keep and boost the user’s excitement to getting a new friend.

Objectives

  • Keep up with the user’s exciment.
  • A faster process.
  • Keep the user updated with process.
  • Enhance communication between the user and the organization.
  • Ensures the user’s comfortability.
  • Provide the user with enough information about the dog.
